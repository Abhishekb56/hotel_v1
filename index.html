ABOUT US

We are here with the dream of bringing great food for the people around. Our Approaches reflects the people we serve.
We are dedicated, passionate, methodical, deliver on promises and yes practical! We believe that a handshake seals
the deal. From the quality of our products to the unparalleled service we provide to our customers, we keep this surety.

The idea behind Dosa Corner is to showcase different variety of dosa seen in all parts of India under one roof
Each taste brings you those memories we had in our traditionally rich past.

Dosa Corner presents some of the best dosa flavours around and deliver honestly with the real tastes.
Our aim is to excite you completely. We consider Dosa Corner to be a truly Indian restaurant that honors
all different tastes of dosa. All customers and staff are considered as an integral part of the restaurant
and without them we are not complete. Our ultimate goal is to make our guests feel relaxed, nourished
and satisfied at all levels of our services and food.
